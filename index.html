About The Umopen

The Umstead Open Invitational is a outdoor event focused on setting goals and finishing them. Whether you want to run 10 miles or bike 100 feet, whatever your goal is, you can accomplish it at The Umopen.

The rules are simple. Kind of.





1. Choose a race buddy/team.

The Umopen is a group event. Each participant must start, and finish, together with their race buddy or team.

A team must have 2 participants, but does not have a max.

2. Choose a distance.

Together with a buddy, you must choose a distance in which to compete. The distance requirement is completely open-ended and up to your team.

3. Choose your format.

Just because you've selected a distance of 10 miles, doesn't mean that each member of your race must complete that 10 miles while running.

At The Umopen each participant can determine what mix of running/biking/swimming/walking/etc they'd like to combine to complete their target distance.

The only requirement is that you must all start and end together. What happens in-between is your team's perogative.
